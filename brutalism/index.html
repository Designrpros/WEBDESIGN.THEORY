Design Concept 01

BRUTALISM

The Philosophy

Raw. Bold. Unapologetic. Design that refuses to apologize for existing.

The Aesthetic

Against polish. Against consistency. For function.

Learn HistoryView Code
History

THE ORIGINS

1950s Architecture

Brutalism began with raw concrete (béton brut) in Le Corbusier's philosophy. Honest materials. Exposed structure.

2010s Web

Designers rebel against flat design. Embrace rawness. Craigslist energy.

Key Traits

WHAT DEFINES IT

Heavy Borders

Thick 4-8px borders that define and frame every element.

border-[4px] border-black shadow-[8px_8px_0_#000]

Bold Typography

Chunky uppercase fonts. Think Impact, Arial Black, system-ui.

font-black uppercase tracking-tighter

Hard Shadows

No blur. 8-12px offset shadows that feel tactile.

shadow-[8px_8px_0_#000] hover:shadow-[12px_12px_0_#000]

Primary Colors

Yellow, red, black, white. No gradients. No pastels.

bg-yellow-400 bg-red-600 bg-black text-white

RULES

DO
✓Use thick borders everywhere
✓Push typography to extremes
✓Embrace primary colors
✓Add hard offset shadows
✓Break grid intentionally
DON'T
✗Use gradients or soft shadows
✗Apply thin or delicate fonts
✗Hide the structure
✗Add unnecessary decoration
✗Round the corners
